      • So, as most of you already know, for an event to be
      classified as a sport it needs to have certain qualities.
      Such qualities can consist of playing on a team, and
      requiring skill. Band is technically classified as a sport,
      however, if you ask many people who are in band, they will
      tell you that it is not a sport. It is true that band does
      require skill and a team, but personally I think that it can
      not be declared a sport if it does not have physical contact.
      And since this is soemething that lacks in marching
      band then a I do not consider it a real sport.
