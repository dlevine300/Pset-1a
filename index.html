Qualificaitons of a Sport

  1. physical contact
  2. A team event
  3. Requiring skill

    Home page 2 page 3 page 4 page 5

So, as most of you already know, for an event to be
classified as a sport it needs to have certain qualities.
Such qualities can consist of having physical contact
in the sport, playing on a team, and requiring skill. While
it is true that band does require skill and a team,
it does not have the physical contact. This
means that marching band can't be a real sport.
